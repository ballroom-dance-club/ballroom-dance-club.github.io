Membership¶

The Ballroom Dance Club is open to everyone who has a passion for dancing! In order to participate in the club's activities, you can either pay a membership fee per the table below or pay a per-visit cost of $5. It's common for dancers to pay the per-visit cost the first time or two and then buy a membership when they know they'll be coming often!

Membership fees cover the cost of our Level 1 and Level 2 lessons. These lessons are intended for beginners and intermediate dancers and are often taught by current members of the University of Michigan Ballroom Dance Team. Membership does not cover our Level 3 lessons, which are $5 per lesson. We make an effort to bring in more advanced instructors for these lessons and our hope is that they challenge you while teaching you a lot to improve your dancing! If there is an instructor you would like us to invite to teach, please let us know!
